# FitClass Brand Guidelines

## Brand Positioning
**Luxury fitness for busy professionals in India.**

### Mission Statement
> *Experience fitness without compromise. Elite training, zero wait time, luxury recovery — for those who value their time as much as their health.*

### Brand Values
- **Efficiency**: Your time is precious. Every detail optimized.
- **Excellence**: Premium in every touchpoint.
- **Experience**: Calm, refined, world-class.

---

## Visual Identity

### Logo
- **Primary**: FitClass wordmark in Inter Bold
- **Usage**: Keep it simple, geometric, timeless
- **Minimum space**: 20px clear zone around logo
- **Approved backgrounds**: Charcoal, off-white, gold accent

### Color Palette

| Color | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|
| **Charcoal** | `#1A1A1A` | Primary background, text |
| **Off-White** | `#F8F7F4` | Secondary background, cards |
| **Warm Gray** | `#6B6B6B` | Body text, subtle elements |
| **Gold** | `#C9A962` | Accent, CTAs, highlights |
| **Deep Gold** | `#A68B4B` | Hover states, emphasis |

### Typography

| Element | Font | Weight | Size |
|---------|------|--------|------|
| H1 Headlines | Inter | 700 | 48-72px |
| H2 Section titles | Inter | 600 | 32-40px |
| H3 Card titles | Inter | 600 | 20-24px |
| Body text | Inter | 400 | 16-18px |
| Small/Caption | Inter | 400 | 14px |

**Rules:**
- Maximum 2 font families
- Generous letter-spacing on headings (1-2px)
- Line height: 1.6 for body, 1.2 for headings

---

## Imagery Guidelines

### Photography Style
- High-end lifestyle shots, minimal clutter
- Real people in authentic poses (not stock)
- Cinematic lighting, muted tones
- Generous negative space

### What to avoid
- Bright, saturated colors
- Cluttered compositions
- Cheesy fitness poses
- Low-quality or pixelated images

---

## Tone of Voice

### Voice Attributes
- **Calm**: No shouting, no urgency
- **Confident**: We know our value
- **Aspirational**: Inspire without bragging
- **Welcoming**: Exclusive but not elitist

### Copy Guidelines
- Short, benefit-focused sentences
- Focus on experience, service, results
- Avoid promotional language ("Best deal!", "Hurry!")
- Use "you" language, speak to the individual

### Example copy:
- ❌ "JOIN NOW FOR 50% OFF! LIMITED TIME!"
- ✅ "Begin your transformation."

---

## Digital Guidelines

### Website
- Generous whitespace
- Full-bleed hero imagery
- Single primary CTA per section
- Subtle micro-animations
- Smooth scroll behavior

### Social Media
- Same color palette and typography
- Curated lifestyle content
- Member stories and events
- Minimal branded graphics
